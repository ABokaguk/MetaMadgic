Q
I'd like to know about the guidelines
for participating in the magic contest

A

We will announce guidelines for video recording before the start of the preliminary round. Initially, to give a brief overview, you can use any camera, whether it's a DSLR, smartphone camera, etc., for video recording. We will provide information on the positioning and angles of a total of 5 cameras. You must upload the video according to the camera shooting guide to confirm your participation in the preliminary round. Please be aware that failure to follow the guidelines for recording may result in participation restrictions, so please make sure to check the guidelines before participating.

Q
Are there any benefits to being
a premium member?

A

  1. Premium view service allows viewing of magic videos from different angles.
  2. If the magician you voted for wins in the IMC, you earn rewards in MagicCoin.
  3. With IMC voting rights, you can vote a minimum of 5 times and a maximum of 7 times.
  4. Purchase magic items from the Magic Shopping Mall using MagicCoin.

Q
Are there separate qualifications for
participation in the competition?

A

Regardless of whether you are a professional or an amateur, anyone who loves magic from around the world can participate.

Q
During the magic contest,
is only one vote allowed per person?

A

Upon signing up for MetaMagic, free members are granted one basic voting right. By watching advertisements, they can earn up to 3 additional voting rights. Upon upgrading to premium membership, members are granted 5 basic voting rights, with the potential to earn up to 7 additional voting rights by watching advertisements. Each person can only use one voting right per magician they like.

Q
Do I need to install the
Meta Magic platform?

A

Yes, upon installing the MetaMagic platform, you will receive annual IMC voting rights along with the ability to watch magic videos and learn magic tricks uploaded to "Learning Magic." Additionally, you can earn MagicCoin through activities such as watching advertisements.

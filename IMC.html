2025

The first-ever online/offline simultaneous magic competition, IMC
will be held in 2025 on the fantasy island of Jeju Island in South Korea and the dragon city of Hanoi in Asia. This prestigious competition offers large prize pool in the history of magic competitions.
Magicians from around the world, whether amateur or professional, can participate in the competition. Following the provided shooting guidelines, they will film their magic videos and register them on Meta Magic SNS. Customers from around the world will then participate in voting, determining the winners through preliminary and final rounds, followed by an award ceremony.
The winning teams will plan global tour performances every year. IMC, a magic festival where rankings are determined by real-time viewer voting through simultaneous live broadcasts worldwide, is set to become a new stage for magic festivals, collaborating with IT technologies such as 5G communication.

1st Stage

Online Viewer Voting

  • Free membership registration via smartphone app or website
  • Members can vote for their favorite magic team (1 free vote per member, additional voting rights can be earned by watching advertisements, up to a total of 3 votes allowed per member; voting for the same team multiple times by one person is not permitted)

*Ads: Promotions for tourist attractions in Jeju Island and Hanoi, as well as corporate ads

2nd Stage

Judges Evaluation and On-site Voting (Online)

  • Scores from both the judging panel and on-site audience voting (online) are combined within 12 hours after the competition.
  • Teams advancing to the final round will be provided with round-trip airfare and accommodation (except for individual participants)

Scoring Method:
Online Voting 70% + Judges Evaluation 30%

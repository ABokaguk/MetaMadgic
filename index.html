Introduction

MetaMagic is the magical platform service of state-of-the-art IT technology that creates happiness and joy.

With the advancement of smartphones, various streaming and social media platforms
provide video content. Among them, magic is the most beloved content.

MetaMagic allows you to select and distribute magical content on your smartphone,
including world-first magic and exceptional magic performances, thereby generating revenue.

01
Viewer-driven
Magic Content

There is no magic streaming program anywhere in the world where viewers can directly select magicians. MetaMagic is a link that allows users to get closer to the world of magic. You can watch, learn, follow, and distribute never-before-seen magical streams created by world-famous magicians in real-time.

02
Viewer-selected
Magic Contests

In magic contests, viewers can participate in voting. Unlike hidden magic contests by a minority of magicians, contests unfold globally in real-time using blockchain technology, allowing viewers to directly vote for the winners. Scores from around the world are transparently disclosed without deception. If the magician you voted for wins, you can earn a tremendous amount of coins.

03
Magic Prosumer

Become a new concept of prosumer where both those who upload magic videos and those who watch them can earn money. We support you to enjoy and own amazing magic anytime, anywhere with just a smartphone. We provide a new concept service where you can earn money just by watching videos.

the World's Largest Magic Shop

You can earn money by directly distributing the world's best magic tools and introducing videos to learn. Even if you are not a magician, you become the owner of an amazing magic market where you can make money with magic. You receive revenue just by introducing it on social media.

06
Magic Communication

07
Magic Coin System

Through charging Reward Points and Magic Coins, virtual currencies, users can use them within the platform like cash, and through the Magic Exchange, it becomes possible to exchange them for global coins such as Bitcoin or Ethereum. Users can stay within the MetaMagic platform, securing Reward Points through ad viewing and participating in various promotions, completing a circular coin system using them.

08
Hybrid Revenue System Based
on NFT-Clipto Format

MetaMagic platform provides a differentiated live commerce solution. Magicians and commerce users share videos they upload on other platforms (SNS, ZALO, TICTIK, YouTube, etc.), and a certain profit rate in MP is provided for the revenue generated from such shared content. Through this, MetaMagic users can become online marketers, enabling revenue generation for anyone. It is a platform where not only content creators but also delivery users distributing the content can generate more revenue.

Magic Coin System

Meta Magic (MM) operates the Magic Coin (MC) system. Within MM, it encourages the use of Magic Points (MP) instead of cards or cash. Users can recharge MP at the Magic Charging Station and collect reward MP through various promotional activities. Accumulated MP in the electronic wallet can be utilized to purchase magic props, daily necessities, fashion items, goods (vouchers), etc., from the Magic Shopping Mall. Additionally, users can promote their channels, purchase thousands of paid contents and subscriptions at the International Magic Carnival (IMC), and help those in need through the donation center.



Furthermore, at the Magic Coin Exchange, users can convert their held MPoints into MCoin, which can then be exchanged for cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc. Through this, Meta Magic (MM) will also serve as a more efficient global hyper-chain-based coin exchange center.
